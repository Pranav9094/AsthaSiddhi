FAQ

Common Questions

Are AshtaSiddhi products safe for long-term use?

add

Yes. All our formulations use classical Ayurvedic herbs in their natural ratios, with no synthetic additives. They are designed for sustained, long-term use. For specific health conditions, we recommend consulting our in-house Vaidya before starting a regimen.

How do I know which product is right for me?

add

We offer a free 15-minute consultation with our certified Ayurvedic physician via phone or WhatsApp. You can also use our Dosha Quiz on the website to get a personalised recommendation based on your body type and primary health concerns.

Do you offer COD and easy returns?

add

Yes — Cash on Delivery is available across India. We also offer a 30-day satisfaction guarantee. If you are not satisfied with the product for any reason, contact our support team and we will issue a full refund or replacement, no questions asked.

How long before I see results?

add

Ayurveda works gently and sustainably. Most customers notice significant improvements within 4-6 weeks of consistent use. Acute conditions may show relief sooner (1-2 weeks), while chronic conditions typically require 2-3 months for full results.

Are your products certified and tested?

add

Absolutely. AshtaSiddhi is GMP-certified and our products undergo third-party testing at NABL-accredited laboratories. Each batch is tested for heavy metal limits, microbial contamination, pesticide residues, and active compound concentration. CoA reports are available on request.
