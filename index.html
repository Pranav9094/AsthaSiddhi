FAQ

Common Questions

Are AshtaSiddhi products safe for long-term use?

add

Yes. All our formulations use classical Ayurvedic herbs in their natural ratios, with no synthetic additives. They are designed for sustained, long-term use. For specific health conditions, we recommend consulting our in-house Vaidya before starting a regimen.

How do I know which product is right for me?

add

We offer a free consultation with our certified Ayurvedic physician via phone or WhatsApp. You can also contact us with your health concerns and our team will recommend the most suitable formulation for your needs.

How long before I see results?

add

Ayurveda works gently and sustainably. Most customers notice significant improvements within 4–6 weeks of consistent use. Acute conditions may show relief sooner (1–2 weeks), while chronic conditions typically require 2–3 months for full results.

Are your products certified and tested?

add

Absolutely. AshtaSiddhi is GMP & ISO certified. Products undergo third-party testing at NABL-accredited laboratories for heavy metals, microbial contamination, pesticide residues, and active compound concentration. CoA reports are available on request.

Do you offer wholesale / B2B supply?

add

Yes. We supply to pharmacies, wellness clinics, Ayurvedic practitioners, and distributors across India. Please contact us via email or WhatsApp for bulk pricing, minimum order quantities, and distribution partnership details.
